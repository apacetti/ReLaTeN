LATeN Seminar

Castellano | English

The Seminario Latinoamericano de Teoría de Números (LATeN) starts in the year 2020 during the COVID pandemic. The goal of the seminar is to promote the development of Number Theory in Latin America. Also, to create a network between its researchers, via regular research presentations, together with a virtual coffee at the end of each presentation. The seminar run until 2022, and was a huge success. Most presentations are recorded, and available at the seminar official website.
