AGRA Schools

Castellano | English

The AGRA schools (Arithmetic, Groups and Analysis) have as main goal to contribute with the development of Number Theory (in the broadest sense) as an area of research in Latin America. The schools allow young students/researchers to undertake short research projects with experts in the area. Each event consists on five courses, together with problem/research sessions during two weeks. The courses topics include analytic number theory, algebraic number theory, Diophantine geometry and combinatorics (among others).
